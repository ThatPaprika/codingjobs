
When you go to a page on your browser :
1. You're making a request to the server
2. The server will understand which page you want
3. Server will give you back the page you want
4. Browser will display the page

--------

When you go to a bar/restaurant and ask for a cocktail/food : 
1. You're asking the waiter for your cocktail
2. The waiter will look for your cocktail in the kitchen/bar
3. The waiter will you give you back what you asked for
4. Enjoy your drink.
